Description

Soybean meal (Soya De Oiled Cake) is the byproduct of the extraction of soybean oil. It is globally the primary protein supplement used in livestock and poultry diets. It has an excellent amino acid profile and high digestibility. Our Soya meal is processed from non-GMO seeds using the solvent extraction method, ensuring maximum protein retention and minimal oil residue.
