Description

Sesame is one of the oldest oilseed crops known, domesticated well over 3000 years ago. It has one of the highest oil contents of any seed. With a rich, nutty flavor, it is a common ingredient in cuisines across the world. Our Natural Sesame Seeds are sourced from the best growing regions in India and processed under strict hygiene conditions.
