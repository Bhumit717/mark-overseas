Description

Groundnut, also known as Peanut, is one of the important oilseeds and food crops. India is one of the largest producers and exporters of Peanuts in the world. We offer High Protein Shelled/Hand Picked Selection (HPS) peanuts. We supply Bold and Java varieties, which are consumed directly as a snack or used in food industries. Our quality checks ensure they are free from aflatoxin and other contaminants.
