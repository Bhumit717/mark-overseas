Description

Bishop's weed or ajwain is a very popular Indian spice used to add flavor to many dishes. It has been used in Ayurvedic medicine as an antiseptic, spice, and preservative. Used for respiratory and GI ailments.

The seeds have a bitter and pungent taste with a flavor similar to anise and oregano. They smell almost exactly like thyme because they contain thymol, but are more aromatic and less subtle in taste.

Our Bishop's Weed is sourced from the best regions in India and processed under strict hygiene conditions to maintain quality and freshness.
