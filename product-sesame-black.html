Description

Black sesame seeds are small, flat, oily seeds that grow in the fruit pods of the Sesamum indicum plant. They have been harvested for thousands of years. Black sesame seeds are richer in flavor and have a stronger aroma than white sesame seeds. They are an excellent source of calcium, iron, and magnesium. Our seeds are carefully cleaned and sorted to ensure only the highest quality black seeds are exported.
