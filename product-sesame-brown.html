Description

Brown sesame seeds are similar to white ones but are unhulled and possess a slightly more bitter and complex flavor. They are exceptionally rich in calcium and magnesium. Our brown sesame seeds undergo multi-stage cleaning to ensure they are free from foreign matter and uniform in quality.
