Description

Green Cardamom is the small seed pod of the cardamom plant. It's harvested when green and then dried. Cardamom has a strong, unique taste, with an intensely aromatic, resinous fragrance. It's commonly used in Indian cuisine, Middle Eastern dishes, and for flavoring coffee and tea. We ensure only the freshest pods are selected to retain the maximum volatile oil content.
