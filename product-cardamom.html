Cardamom (Small & Large)

Cardamom, the 'Queen of Spices', is one of the world's most expensive and flavored spices. We offer both the delicate Green (Small) variety and the robust, smoky Black (Large) variety.

Description

Small (Green) Cardamom: Harvested from Elettaria cardamomum, it is recognized by its small green pods and is prized for its intense, sweet, and floral flavor. It is a staple in desserts, masalas, and beverages.

Large (Black) Cardamom: Sourced from Amomum subulatum, these pods are larger and dark brown/black. They have a strong, smoky, and camphor-like flavor, often used in hearty savory dishes like biryanis and stews.
