Description

India is the global leader in rice production, offering the finest Basmati and Non-Basmati varieties. Our Basmati Rice, known as the 'Scented Pearl', features extra-long grains that elongate twice their size upon cooking with a heavenly aroma. Non-Basmati varieties like IR64 and Sona Masoori offer excellent texture for daily consumption. All our rice is well-milled, 100% Sortex cleaned, and free from polishing agents, ensuring nutritional integrity and superior taste.
