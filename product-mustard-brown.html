Description

Brown mustard seeds are smaller and more pungent than the yellow variety. They are a staple in Indian tempering (tadka) and global condiment manufacturing. Our brown mustard seeds are sourced from fertile regions, ensuring a high concentration of essential oils that provide a sharp, characteristic bite.
