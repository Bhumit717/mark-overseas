Description

Rice Bran De Oiled Cake (DOC) is obtained by extracting the oil from fresh rice bran. It is a highly nutritious livestock feed ingredient, especially for ruminant animals and aquaculture. It is rich in proteins and contains a good amount of phosphorus. Our Rice Bran DOC is processed to ensure low sand and silica content, making it safe and highly beneficial for animal health and growth.
