Description

Indian Groundnuts (Peanuts) are known for their sweet flavor and high nutritional content. We supply both Bold (Runner) and Java (Spanish) varieties, shelled or in-shell. The Bold variety is larger and ideal for roasting, while Java is smaller and preferred for confectionery. Our groundnuts are strictly tested for Aflatoxin (B1/B2/G1/G2) to meet stringent EU and USA standards.
