Description

Indian Red Chilli is globally celebrated for its unique pungency, vibrant color, and medicinal properties. Primarily grown in the fertile soils of Guntur (Andhra Pradesh) and Byadgi (Karnataka), these chillies are a staple in international cuisines. We offer premium varieties including S17 Teja for extreme heat and Byadgi for deep natural color. Our chillies are carefully sun-dried, machine-cleaned, and Sortex-graded to meet global safety standards (IPM and Non-IPM).
