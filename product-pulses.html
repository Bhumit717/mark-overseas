Description

Pulses are a low-fat source of protein with high fiber content and a low glycemic index. They are an essential part of a balanced diet across many cultures. Our range includes high-quality Chickpeas (Kabuli), various Lentils (Dal), and specialty beans like Black Matpe. We ensure that our pulses are cleaned of any foreign material and are graded for size and color to ensure consistent cooking quality.
