Description

Indian Fennel Seeds (Saunf) are distinguished by their sweet, anise-like flavor and cooling properties. Grown mainly in Gujarat and Rajasthan, we provide Lucknowi (Thin) and Bold varieties. Our seeds are machine-cleaned and Sortex-sorted to ensure zero impurities. They are widely used as a digestif, culinary spice, and in the formulation of herbal medicines.
