Description

Roasted Gram, also known as Bhuna Chana, is a cornerstone of healthy Indian snacking. It is prepared by roasting whole chickpeas under controlled temperatures to achieve the perfect crunch without losing nutritional value. It is an excellent source of plant-based protein, dietary fiber, and essential minerals like iron and manganese. Our roasted gram is sourced from premium harvests and processed in state-of-the-art facilities to ensure zero moisture and maximum freshness. It is widely used in making traditional snacks, chutneys, and as a protein-rich addition to modern diets.
