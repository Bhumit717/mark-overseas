Description

Yellow and Brown Mustard seeds from India are prized for their pungent flavor and oil-rich properties. Yellow (Sinapis alba) is milder and preferred for condiments, while Brown (Brassica juncea) has medium-high heat. Our seeds are machine-cleaned and handpicked (HPS) to ensure zero foreign matter. They are an essential ingredient in spice blends, mustard pastes, and traditional pickling.
