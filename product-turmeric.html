Description

Turmeric is a rhizomatous herbaceous perennial plant of the ginger family. It is widely used in cooking and as a powerful coloring agent. Beyond the kitchen, it is celebrated in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Our turmeric is carefully dried and polished to maintain its vibrant yellow-orange color and therapeutic value.
