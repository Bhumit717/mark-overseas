Description

Indian Sesame Seeds are highly valued for their rich oil content (up to 50%) and distinct nutty flavor. We offer Natural, Hulled, and Black Sesame seeds, all processed in state-of-the-art facilities. Our Hulled Sesame is processed using both chemical-free mechanical hulling and salt-water methods. These seeds are a rich source of calcium, iron, and antioxidants, and are widely used in bakery products, tahini, and oil extraction.
