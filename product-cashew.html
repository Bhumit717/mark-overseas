Description

Cashew nuts are nutrient-dense seeds and one of the most popular edible nuts in the world. They are rich in healthy fats, protein, and essential minerals. We source raw cashew nuts and process them using the traditional steam-softening method to ensure the kernels remain whole and retain their natural nutrients. Our W240 (King of Cashews) and W320 are highly sought after in international markets.
