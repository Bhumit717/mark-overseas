Description

Indian Pomegranates, especially the Bhagwa variety, are highly demanded in European and Asian markets. They are characterized by their thick, glossy skin which protects the delicate arils inside. Sourced primarily from Maharashtra and Gujarat, they provide a rich source of Vitamin C and antioxidants. Our process involves cleaning, grading by size, and packing in ventilated boxes to ensure optimal quality upon arrival.
